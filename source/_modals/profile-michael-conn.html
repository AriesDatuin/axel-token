Michael Conn

Michael was the co-Founder and CEO of Ether Capital (ETHC:NEO) having successfully led a recent $45 million private placement raise and subsequent reverse takeover to go public. He is also the Founder of Quail Creek Partners, an advisory and consulting business focused on the global financial services, financial technology and blockchain/crypto space. Michael brings a wealth of experience in financial services having served in various senior executive positions at AllianceBernstein and The TCW Group, Inc., with responsibilities for portfolio management, operations, sales, and marketing. Michael’s diverse industry experience includes time at the Los Angeles Times/Tribune Company, McMaster-Carr Supply Company, and the US Embassy in Paris. Michael, who is fluent in French and conversant in several other languages, holds a Master’s degree in International Finance and Economics from Brandeis University and an MBA with honors from the University of Southern California’s Marshall School of Business.
