Ben Ow

Ben has a strong history of leading the creation of stunning, industry-shaking technologies. He started and cultivated a one-man operation into a powerhouse computing company that represented brands like Panasonic, Fujitsu, and Acer. He's co-founded several successful businesses, held executive leadership positions in more, and is the mastermind behind ViaCube, the winner of CES's 2006 Best Of Innovation Award. He also completed all course work for the PHD program in Artificial Intelligence at Northwestern University.
